2.5 MW Pulsed Power Supply

This project is a 1ms, 120 pulses per second, 2.5MW pulsed power supply that is called a modulator and is used to drive high power klystrons. The team studied and designed a low power prototype for proof of concept on a particular circuit topology. These machines in particular have a niche market, with the US sharing about half the customers with European companies.
